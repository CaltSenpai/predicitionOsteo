Understanding Osteoarthritis

Learn about this common joint condition and how AI can help with early detection

üîç What is Osteoarthritis?

Osteoarthritis is the most common form of arthritis, affecting millions of people worldwide. It occurs when the protective cartilage that cushions the ends of bones wears down over time, leading to pain, stiffness, and reduced mobility.

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Symptoms

  • Joint pain and stiffness, especially in the morning
  • Reduced range of motion and flexibility
  • Swelling and tenderness around joints
  • Bone spurs around affected joints
  • Grinding sensation during movement
  • Joint instability and weakness

üéØ Risk Factors

  • Age (risk increases significantly after 50)
  • Obesity and excess weight
  • Previous joint injuries or trauma
  • Repetitive stress on joints from work/sports
  • Genetic predisposition and family history
  • Bone deformities or joint malformation
